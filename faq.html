FAQ

Have a general question about MugCatStudios? Get it answered here! Still clueless? Contact us! Your question might be added to this list.

  • Who runs MugCatStudios? It is run by a bunch of teens. You can find a list on the home page.
  • What genres of movies does your studio produce? We make exiting movies for teens, full of teamwork and conflict! Although it is hard to pinpoint exact genres because the MugCatStudios team likes lots of different types of movies, I would say we do drama, action, thriller...
  • How can I audition for a role in one of your films? Just contact us! If we are specifically looking for actors it will say that on the movies page, but even if we don't we will still consider your offer.
  • Where can I watch your movies? Go to page the Movies page and find a movie you like, then click the "Learn more here!" link. Methods of watching will be found there.
  • Do you accept scripts from outside writers? Sure! Contact us with your idea. If we accept, you can help with the movie. You might even want to permanently join the team!
